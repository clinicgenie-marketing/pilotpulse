# Project Website for PilotPulse

## Goal
Generate qualified demo and consultation requests by helping operational businesses adopt agentic AI inside real workflows.

## Audience
- Operations leaders and general managers
- Founders and Group CEOs
- Functional heads (customer support, recruitment/HR, sales ops)
- SMEs and mid-market teams in logistics, healthcare, recruitment, catering, manufacturing, professional services and trade

## CTA
Identify Your First AI Workflow
(secondary: Request a Demo / See Real Implementations)

---

## Pages (7 total)

### 1. Landing page (`/`)
The primary conversion page and the only full marketing page. Section order:
1. Hero — "Your business is already running on AI. Just not yours."
2. IMDA credibility band
3. Reality & tension — "This is what operations still look like today"
4. Core positioning — "AI fails everywhere except inside real operations" + human-first
5. Capability cards — "AI agents supporting real business workflows"
6. Proven implementations — named case-study cards (outcomes shown inline; full stories live on the separate blog)
7. Smart design for measurable ROI — Clarity / Focus / Momentum
8. Three ways to build AI advantage — service tiers
9. Why PilotPulse — "Built for real operational workflows"
10. Our values
11. From opportunity to implementation — 5-step process
12. Testimonials
13. Final CTA — "Start with one workflow"
14. Footer

Note: there is no separate Solutions or Case Studies page. Capabilities, service tiers and proof all live as sections within the landing page. "View case study" links point out to the separate blog.

### 2. Contact Us (`/contact`)
Demo and consultation request forms; location, contact details, opening hours. Primary destination for all CTAs.

### 3. About Us (`/about`)
Mission, human-first philosophy, "partner not vendor" stance, team, IMDA partner status.

### 4. Blog (separate / external)
Hosted separately from the main site. Linked from the nav, footer and every "View case study" link, where the fuller success stories live. Treated as an outbound link, not an internal page.

### 5. Terms & Conditions (`/terms`)
Terms of use.

### 6. Privacy (`/privacy`)
Privacy policy. (Note: fix the unfinished privacy retention clause flagged in research.)

### 7. Error 404 (`/404`)
On-brand dark-theme not-found page with a short message and links back to the landing page and Contact.
