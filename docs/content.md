## Proven implementations

**Heading**
Already working inside real businesses

**Sub-heading**
These are not demos. They are AI agents running in production, built around real SOPs and measured by outcomes.

**Card 1 — Customer Support & Engagement**
*Industry:* Logistics · **Pick Network** (nationwide parcel locker and delivery network)
As customer enquiries surged, the AI resolved tracking and locker issues on WhatsApp — including reading photos of tracking IDs.
*Outcome:* Support costs reduced to roughly one-third.
*CTA:* View case study

**Card 2 — Scheduling & Follow-ups**
*Industry:* Healthcare / Aesthetics · **SkinScape Clinic** (Singapore aesthetics clinic, high enquiry volume)
As patient chats grew, the AI handled bookings, confirmations and follow-ups so staff could focus on care.
*Outcome:* Fewer missed appointments.
*CTA:* View case study

**Card 3 — Sales Enquiry & Recommendation**
*Industry:* Catering · **Bijan Catering** (corporate catering brand)
During peak periods, the AI clarified requirements and recommended suitable menus on WhatsApp before handing over to staff.
*Outcome:* Faster replies and higher conversion.
*CTA:* View case study

**Card 4 — Recruitment Screening**
*Industry:* Recruitment · **Henderson Recruitment** (security and blue-collar hiring)
With high inbound volume, the AI screened eligibility, answered role questions and scheduled interviews using live hiring criteria.
*Outcome:* Up to 25 minutes saved per candidate.
*CTA:* View case study

**Card 5 — Candidate Matching**
*Industry:* HR services · **Connect Energy Services** (specialist roles)
Facing large CV volumes, the AI matched profiles to job requirements and produced client-ready summaries for review.
*Outcome:* Up to 5× more CVs screened, with consistent evaluation.
*CTA:* View case study

**Card 6 — Operations Automation**
*Industry:* Operations / SAP · **SuperWorld Electronics**
The AI supports a previously manual SAP workflow, reducing repetitive updates and data-entry errors.
*Outcome:* Operational team freed for higher-value work; fewer costly data errors.
*CTA:* View case study
